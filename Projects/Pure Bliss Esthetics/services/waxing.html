Important Note:

Discontinue retinol and other prescription topicals 7-10 days prior to waxing or a facial. These medications can cause the skin to thin or be extra sensitive, leading to potential skin lifting during waxing. If you have taken Accutane within the last year, it is not advised to wax for one year after stopping this medication. Please discontinue the following medications at least 7-10 days prior to any waxing services:

  • Retin A (all types including Alustra, Avage, Avita, Renova, Tazarac, Tazarotene, Tretinoin)
  • Acne medications (Adapalene, Differin, Isotretinoin, Tazorac)
  • Antibiotics (Clindamycin, Doxycycline, Erythromycin, Madifloxicine, Metronidazole, Minocycline, Tetracycline)
