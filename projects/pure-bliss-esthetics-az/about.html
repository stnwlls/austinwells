Meet Piper Bucher

I am a solo aesthetician with a strong passion for all things skin and brows. My journey began with my own skin struggles, inspiring me to help others achieve the same confidence and comfort in their skin. With 7 years of experience in the field, I specialize in a variety of skincare and waxing services, including massage-focused customized facials using Eminence Organic Skincare, dermaplaning, back treatments, brow waxing and tinting, microneedling, and more.

In my free time, I enjoy nature hiking and being outside, a true lover of the sun. I love my cats and cherish moments spent in nature. While many estheticians might discourage sun exposure, I believe in helping my clients enjoy life in the sun safely. I provide guidance on how to protect their skin while still embracing the outdoors.

I believe that every appointment should be a relaxing experience, not just a treatment. Whether you're getting a facial or an eyebrow wax, my goal is to ensure you leave feeling rejuvenated and stress-free. I don't want my services to feel like a doctor's appointment; instead, I aim to create a serene environment where you can fully relax and decompress.

My philosophy is simple: use only organic and all-natural products, and provide an honest, personal touch to every service.

Certifications and Expertise

I hold an esthetician license, certifications with Eminence for the products I use, and a collagen induction therapy certification for microneedling. My commitment to using organic and natural products ensures that I only use what I truly believe in and what I would use on myself. I do not partner with companies for paid promotions, so you can trust that everything I use is true to my values and my business.

Why Eminence Organic Skincare?

Eminence Organic Skincare stands out due to its dedication to natural, potent ingredients. They harvest plants at the peak of their season to ensure the nutrients remain in their most potent form, providing the best possible results for skincare. Their commitment to organic and Biodynamic® farming practices ensures that the products are not only effective but also environmentally sustainable. Eminence is also deeply committed to social responsibility through initiatives like the Eminence Kids Foundation. This nonprofit organization provides organic foods to seriously ill children in hospitals and long-term care facilities worldwide, aiding in their recovery and healing. This mission resonates with my values and the care I wish to extend to my clients.
